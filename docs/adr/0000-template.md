# ADR: Title

## Status

State whether this ADR is proposed, accepted, superseded, or rejected.

## Date

Record the date the ADR is proposed or accepted.

## Owners

List the maintainer or contributors responsible for driving the decision.

## Context

Describe the problem, constraints, threat model, prior decisions, and relevant security invariants.

## Decision

State the decision clearly. Include what will change and what will not change.

## Security considerations

Explain how this decision affects trust boundaries, runner capabilities, evidence integrity, policy behavior, credentials, network access, sanitization, or user-facing security claims.

## Alternatives considered

List meaningful alternatives and why they were not chosen.

## Consequences

Describe expected benefits, costs, risks, and tradeoffs.

## Operational and migration impact

Describe rollout steps, compatibility concerns, migration work, rollback options, and operational responsibilities.

## Validation plan

Describe tests, reviews, experiments, threat-model updates, or other evidence needed to validate the decision.

## References

Link to relevant issues, pull requests, specifications, documentation, experiments, or prior ADRs.
